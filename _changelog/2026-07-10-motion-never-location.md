title: Motion, never location
date: 2026-07-10
categories: [design, learn]
tags: [privacy, engineering]

Most fitness apps can infer where you live because every route begins at your door. Mosey does not request location permission at all.

Mosey only needs step count and cadence to make playback responsive. Those signals stay on the device. There is no route to upload because no route is ever recorded.
