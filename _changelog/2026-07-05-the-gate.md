The first complete loop is running: the pedometer feeds the playback state machine, which fades the podcast in when motion begins and out after a short grace period when motion stops.

Traffic lights stay forgiving. Treadmills work because the system watches steps rather than distance. Most importantly, the phone can stay in your pocket.
